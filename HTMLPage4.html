The importance of artificial intelligence in business

Artificial intelligence helps enhance business capabilities in all areas, and gives companies the ability to demonstrate all their capabilities and raise them to the highest levels. It increases the efficiency of business and the speed of its implementation, increases its value, contributes to the continuous development of business, and increases the number of interacting with these businesses, due to the continuous development of tools and software related to it.

The importance of artificial intelligence in daily life

- The use of artificial intelligence applications has revolutionized the automotive industry. For example, Google's self-driving program uses artificial intelligence techniques, as do logistics companies such as Uber; To reduce accidents and reduce traffic congestion. - Artificial intelligence applications are used in e-commerce websites; Like Amazon, to get a clear picture of customers' behavior in purchases on the site, and to make recommendations. - Social networks use artificial intelligence applications, such as: Facebook AI, to detect the presence of a breach of user photos.
